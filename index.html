Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the AI in detecting dance movements?

Our AI utilizes state-of-the-art computer vision technology and has been trained on millions of dance movements. It achieves an accuracy rate of over 95% in detecting and analyzing dance poses and movements across various styles.

Can Dancer.ai be used for any dance style?

Yes! Dancer.ai is designed to work with a wide range of dance styles, including ballet, contemporary, hip-hop, ballroom, and many more. Our AI is continuously learning and adapting to new dance forms.

How does the AI judging assistance ensure fairness?

Our AI judging system uses objective criteria based on technique, timing, and artistic expression. It's trained on diverse datasets to minimize bias and provides consistent scoring across all participants. Human judges can use this as a reference to ensure fair evaluations.
